Distribution of Basic Amenities

Date: 12th and 13th April

GYWS collaborated with the Gram Panchayat to create a team of 15 -20 volunteers which consisted of local villagers and panchayat members for the door to door distribution of basic amenities to the rural households in the area under Gopali gram panchayat covering nearly 2000 families during the difficult situation of COVID-19.

Following items were distributed to each household:
  • Rice - 3kg
  • Potatoes - 2kg
  • Onions - 0.5kg
  • Nutrela Soyabean - 100g
  • Sanitizers


Collaboration with
Zomato Feeding India

(700 food kits)

700 food kits comprising of 10kg rice and 2 kgs pulses were received from Zomato Feeding India which were distributed in the villages around IIT Kharagpur.

The kits were received on 23rd May 2020 and distribution happened from 24th May to 15th June.


Beneficiaries Served



211 families of the students of JVM

200 families of Tribal groups residing in the villages of Kalaikundi, Hiradi, and Talbagicha.

108 kits were distributed in Salua, Naya Basti, and Purana Basti in an Open Distribution to the families of laborers and daily wage workers.

170 kits were given to the families living in the villages of Kalaikunda, Dompara, Salikxa, and Majhi Para. 14 were given to residents of Panchberia.
